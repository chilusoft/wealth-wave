## Detailed Privacy Statement

### 1. Data Collection

**Wealth Wave does NOT collect any data whatsoever.**

We do not:
- Collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information
- Store your SMS messages on any server
- Track your location
- Record your transaction history remotely
- Use analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Employ any tracking mechanisms
- Connect to any cloud services
- Send any data to any third party
- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

### 2. How Your Data is Processed

**Everything happens on YOUR device:**

1. **SMS Reading**: The app reads SMS from mobile money providers (MoMo, AirtelMoney, StanChart) using Android's SMS permission
2. **Local Processing**: Transaction information is extracted and processed entirely on your device
3. **Local Storage**: All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device only
4. **Zero Transmission**: No data is ever transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party

### 3. Internet Connection

**Wealth Wave does NOT require an internet connection to function.**

- ‚úÖ The app works 100% offline
- ‚úÖ No data is sent over the internet
- ‚úÖ You can block the app with a mobile firewall and it will still work perfectly
- ‚úÖ You can use the app in airplane mode
- ‚úÖ No background data transmission occurs

**Test it yourself:** Block Wealth Wave's internet access using any mobile firewall app (NetGuard, AFWall+, etc.) and verify that all features continue to work normally.

### 4. Permissions Used

Wealth Wave requires only two permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | What We Do | What We DON'T Do |
|------------|---------|------------|------------------|
| **READ_SMS** | To read mobile money transaction SMS | Read only SMS from MoMo, AirtelMoney, and StanChart to detect transactions | We do NOT read personal messages, store SMS content, or access messages from other sources |
| **WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE** | To store transaction database locally | Save your transaction history on your device | We do NOT upload data anywhere or share it with anyone |
